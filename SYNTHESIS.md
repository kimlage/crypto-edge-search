## Polymarket — where the edge is NOT (and here we can prove it: markets resolve)

- **Copy-trading public wallets — KILL.** Performance does not persist; top-decile-train wallets *lose*
  −$90k OOS; the wallet-label-shuffle surrogate is p=0.53 (copying the "best" is no better than random) and
  the no-persistence holds across **5 walk-forward windows** (Stouffer z=−0.13). "70%+ win-rate" is the
  *anti*-signal (it selects longshot-sellers). The viral "Claude + copy-trade = print money" posts are
  recycled marketing + survivorship cherry-picks (the headline numbers are arithmetically self-inconsistent).
- **The market is well-calibrated** in aggregate (price-tied on-winner gap = +0.0001 over 1.36M trades).
- **Favorite-longshot mispricing is real but not deployable** — every fade/buy child dies on the upset tail +
  wide longshot spread; "98% win-rate" selling-longshot bots pass DSR/Sharpe but **fail the right null** (the
  realized mean is below a calibrated market) — the canonical case for why the *complete* gauntlet matters.
- **No riskless arbitrage** (true negRisk baskets carry a +7% overround; within-market complete-set is
  structurally impossible). **No money-management rescue** (sizing is expectancy-sign-invariant; honest Kelly
  bets $0; empirical-q Kelly is look-ahead → ruin). **22 reverse-engineering mechanisms** all KILL/DEFER.
- **Free-information forecasting — KILL, even forward.** The market is better-calibrated than free climatology
  *and*, in a pre-registered forward test, than a real Open-Meteo ensemble forecast (Brier market < forecast).
  Details + the credibility backlog + the honest audit: **[polymarket/](docs/polymarket/)**.

## The meta-conclusion

Of hundreds of hypotheses spanning the full retail/quant arsenal across two very different markets, **none
cleared the full gauntlet on data it had never seen** — and a positive control proves the gauntlet *would*
have found one. The durable deliverable is the **methodology** (the committed chain, the right-null
discipline, honest N, the family-wise surrogate, the consume-once holdout, the financing-honest cost model)
plus the **body of negative evidence** that maps where the edge is not. The two prediction-market and crypto
"real things" — favorite-longshot premium and carry — are sub-cost regime phenomena, not a business for an
individual at retail cost. **No capital is deployed.**
