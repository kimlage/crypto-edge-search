## The project's own conclusion

**Nothing in this repository is deployable.** Across the techniques tested, **zero**
cleared every gate to a tradeable edge; the handful that survived early gates were
flagged too weak to trade and were not promoted. The working hypothesis the data
keeps confirming is that, **for an individual at retail cost, speculation behaves far
more like a game of chance than a reliable way to make money.** We publish what dies
as loudly as what lives precisely because a clean backtest is the cheapest thing in
markets.

If you read a number in this repo as a green light to trade, you have misread it. The
binding result of every analysis here is a **KILL**, a **PROMISING-but-not-deployable**,
or a **DEFERRED** — never a "go".

## Why this exists

The value of this project is the **methodology and the negative results** — an honest,
reproducible record of techniques that do *not* survive scrutiny net of cost on unseen
data. Use it to learn how to falsify a strategy, not to find one to deploy.
