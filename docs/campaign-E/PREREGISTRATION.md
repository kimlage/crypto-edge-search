## 0. Purpose and multiplicity policy

Campaign E is the lab's pre-registered forward family: four hypotheses whose configurations are
frozen **before any forward bar is scored**, with the multiplicity, the look schedule, and the honest
power horizons declared in this document rather than discovered after the fact.

**The family, prospectively: K = 4 hypotheses** — E1, E2, E3, E4 below. Adjudication is
**family-wise** at Bonferroni **α = 0.05 / 4 = 0.0125** per hypothesis, declared now.

**Where the statistical claim starts.** These 4 were selected from a wider adversarially-judged
candidate set (24 candidates, 48 judge verdicts; PROJECT_REVIEW_2026-06-09.md §4). That 24→4
selection was **research triage, not a statistical claim** — no forward data was consumed by it, and
nothing in it is conditioned on the forward window this document governs. The statistical claim
begins **at this freeze**: from the binding commit onward, the family is fixed at K=4, every config
is hash-pinned, and all scoring follows the §5 look schedule. This answers the "selecting 1 of 24
ideas is itself an argmax" critique one level up, exactly as disposed in PROJECT_REVIEW §7.

**Verdict rule.** A hypothesis can end in exactly three states: **KILL** (fails its declared gates at
a scheduled look, or its declared kill condition fires), **SURVIVE** (clears its declared boundary at
a scheduled look under the §5 alpha-spending schedule, family-wise), or **OPEN** (still accruing).
No mid-course config edits, ever — any edit creates a *new* hypothesis and increments the family
denominator (§5).

**No capital deploys before SURVIVE.** Everything here is paper-forward. Data cost is $0 (§6) and
there is no recurring paid infrastructure.

## 1. E1 — XS Donchian canonical forward watch

**Claim.** The pre-registered canonical cross-sectional Donchian channel-position long-short book
earns a positive beta-hedged net mean return on forward data.

**Honest framing first (per §3): this is a KILL-detection / extension watch, not a SURVIVE attempt.**
A 26-week forward window **cannot reach DSR ≥ 0.95 unless the observed annualized Sharpe is ≈ 2.34**
(≈ 2.78 for the t ≥ 1.96 haircut gate) — roughly 5× the lead's honest OOS range of ~0.3–0.5. At true
Sharpe 0.3–0.5 the 80%-power horizon is **31–87 years**. The quarterly looks therefore test the
*kill* condition (decay to ≤ 0 hedged) and extend the prior; any "SURVIVE in 26 weeks" framing would
be a ~2.3-Sharpe fluke and is disclaimed in advance.

### 1.1 Frozen canonical config (extracted verbatim from the repo code)

Source code: `scripts/edgehunt-requeue/donch_ls_final.ts` (signal, weights, costs) and
`scripts/edgehunt-requeue/canon_check.ts`; canonical declaration:
`output/edgehunt-requeue/SUMMARY.md` ("canonical pre-registered config: N=120 zscore HIGH").
Machine-readable copy: `docs/campaign-E/configs/e1-donchian-canonical.json`.

- **Signal — channel position.** For asset *i* at daily close *t*, over the trailing **N = 120**
  daily closes (window `t−119 … t`, inclusive of *t*; all 120 closes must be > 0, channel range
  must exceed 1e−12, else cp is undefined):
  `cp_i(t) = (close_t − min_120) / (max_120 − min_120)`.
- **Variant — HIGH** (`dir = +1`): long high channel position (breakout strength), short low. The
  LOW/reversal direction was the placebo in the original study and is *not* part of this hypothesis.
- **Weights — cross-sectional z-score, dollar-neutral, gross 2.0.** Among assets with a defined cp
  and a tradable next-period return (minimum cross-section: **6** assets, else flat):
  `z_i = (cp_i − mean_cs(cp)) / std_cs(cp)` (std fallback 1 if degenerate), then
  `w_i = 2 · z_i / Σ_j |z_j|` — gross exposure 2.0 (≈1.0 long + ≈1.0 short), dollar-neutral by
  construction. (Code: `donch_ls_final.ts` lines 66–68, the `zscore` family.)
- **Costs.** Taker **4 bps/side** (`COST = 0.0004`) charged on turnover `Σ_i |w_i(t) − w_i(t⁻)|`.
- **Financing (binding).** Borrow charged on the **full short notional** (≈1.0× of the 2.0 gross) at
  a flat, declared **10%/yr APR** (conservative; declared as an assumption to avoid any
  data-dependent rate choice). A 5%/yr sensitivity is *reported* at each look but is non-binding.
- **Rebalance — weekly.** Weights recomputed at the close of the first Monday daily bar (UTC) of
  each ISO week and held for the week. *Spec note:* the backtest code rebalanced daily; the forward
  watch freezes the weekly variant per PROJECT_REVIEW §5B to control turnover cost. This is part of
  the frozen config, declared here, before any forward bar.
- **Universe — live top-30 by trailing 90-day dollar volume**, Binance spot USDT pairs (stablecoin /
  fiat / leveraged-token pairs excluded), refreshed at each weekly rebalance from the most recent
  `market-universe.ndjson` snapshot (recorder §6). Point-in-time by construction — the forward
  universe includes future delistings live, which removes the backtest panel's survivorship caveat.
- **Beta-hedge + gate.** At each weekly rebalance, β of the strategy's *prior realized* daily net
  returns vs (i) BTC and (ii) the equal-weight universe basket is estimated by OLS on the trailing
  **90 calendar days** of **honest-OOS** data (nothing after the rebalance close). A BTC-perp hedge
  overlay of ratio −β_BTC is applied. **Gate (binding): both rolling residual betas must satisfy
  |β| < 0.1** measured at each quarterly look; a breach is recorded as a beta-neutrality gate
  failure for that look.

### 1.2 Pre-commit reference numbers (provenance, not claims)

Replicated 2026-06-09 from the cached panel (`output/crossxs/daily-closes.json`, 30 coins,
2020-06-02 → 2026-05-31) with the exact code above: full-sample net Sharpe **1.4046**, turnover
**0.3853/day** — matching the published canonical (1.405 / 0.385). Audited honest expectations the
watch actually tests against: consume-once holdout DSR@N=1 **0.79**, Newey-West t **0.96**,
borrow-adjusted OOS Sharpe **~0.3–0.5** (EDGE_SEARCH_DOMAIN_CAMPAIGN.md, audit update 2026-06-01).

### 1.3 Kill condition (declared)

At any scheduled look with ≥ 26 weeks of forward data: if the cumulative beta-hedged net mean is
≤ 0 **and** the upper bound of its 95% Newey-West CI is below the audited 0.3-Sharpe-equivalent
mean, E1 is **KILL** (decay confirmed). SURVIVE requires the §5 boundary at family-wise α — per the
table above, realistically a multi-year event.

## 2. E2 — Two-mechanism ensemble (ONE hypothesis)

**Claim.** A fixed-weight ensemble of two mechanism-distinct sleeves — (a) the E1 Donchian book and
(b) the unlevered dated-futures basis-carry book — earns a positive net mean return forward.
**This is one hypothesis**: the ensemble return series is scored, never the sleeves separately
(sleeve diagnostics are reported but carry no verdict weight).

**Sleeve (b) — unlevered dated-basis carry, frozen from the repo code**
(`scripts/edgehunt/dated_futures_carry.ts`, primary committed config, lines 289–296):
long spot + short contango quarterly (BTC + ETH COIN-M), hold to convergence; enter a contract only
when its annualized basis, **net of contemporaneous trailing-30d perp-funding carry**
(`purgePerpAtEntry = true`), exceeds the **6%/yr hurdle** (`annHurdle = 0.06`); exit if basis flips
negative (`exitBackwardation = true`); stack front + next quarter, equal-weight across active legs;
taker 4 bps/side on entry/exit; early-unwind tail charged (daily prob 0.004, 25 bps slip);
**unlevered — 1.0× notional, no leverage** (the levered variant was a financing-leak artifact,
killed by the 2026-06-01 audit). Financing on full notional per §6.

**Frozen ensemble weights — inverse-volatility, 50/50 risk split, pre-commit data only.**
Computed 2026-06-09 from cached pre-commit series only:

| Sleeve | Pre-commit ann. vol | Inverse-vol capital weight |
|---|---:|---:|
| E1 Donchian (gross-2.0 book) | 50.93% (std 0.026657/day, replicated canonical) | **0.0624** |
| Dated-basis unlevered | 3.39% (std 0.0017750/day, `dated_futures_carry_report.json`) | **0.9376** |

Each sleeve contributes ≈ 3.18%/yr vol — an equal-risk 50/50 at ρ ≈ 0. Weights are **frozen**; no
re-estimation, no rebalancing between sleeves beyond restoring the frozen capital weights at each
quarterly look.

**Honest powered horizon, printed bluntly (per §3).** At sleeve Sharpes 0.3–0.7 with ρ ≈ 0, the
ensemble's true Sharpe is ~0.7–0.85, which needs **~11–16 years** of forward data for 80% power.
E2 will not produce a SURVIVE on any practical horizon. Its declared role is **infrastructure +
content**: it exercises the full prereg protocol (frozen weights, recorders, look schedule), it is
the lab's public forward track record, and its quarterly updates are KILL-detection and method
demonstration — not survival theater. The §3 table is the authority for this statement.

**Kill condition.** Ensemble cumulative net mean ≤ 0 at any look with ≥ 2 years of data, or both
sleeves individually KILLed (E1 kill condition; basis sleeve: annualized excess ≤ 0 over ≥ 2 years).

## 3. E3 — Unlock-cliff event study (frozen forward calendar)

**Claim.** Large pre-scheduled investor/team token-unlock cliffs are preceded/accompanied by
negative abnormal returns in the unlocking token relative to BTC, exploitable as a beta-hedged
short over a declared event window.

### 3.1 Frozen forward calendar procedure (the PIT backbone)

- The next-12-month cliff list is compiled from the **free** CryptoRank and Tokenomist token-unlock
  pages and **sha256-frozen** via `scripts/recorders/record-unlock-calendar.mjs`, which writes
  `docs/campaign-E/frozen-calendar-YYYYMMDD.json` (canonical-JSON hash inside).
- **Each event must be verified against the on-chain vesting contract via free public RPCs BEFORE
  inclusion** (contract address, unlock timestamp, amount; verification block recorded per event).
  Unverifiable events are excluded, not guessed.
- **Historical leg explicitly out of scope:** DefiLlama `/emissions` is paywalled (HTTP 402,
  verified live 2026-06-09). The honest $0 version is forward-only; the frozen calendar + passing
  events become the first open PIT-honest unlock dataset, citable independently of the verdict.
- Calendar additions are append-only (new freeze file, new hash); an event already inside its entry
  window at freeze time is ineligible.

### 3.2 Frozen trade config (canonical, binding)

- **Eligibility:** investor/team **cliff** unlocks ≥ **3% of circulating supply** at event date;
  token must have a Binance USDT-M perp listed and trading at entry; event verified per §3.1.
- **Position:** short the token perp; long BTC perp sized **β × short notional**, β = rolling 90-day
  OLS beta of token daily log returns vs BTC estimated with data through **T−11** only.
- **Window:** enter at the **T−10** daily close, exit at the **T+2** daily close (T = unlock
  timestamp's UTC date).
- **Costs:** taker 4 bps/side on each leg; perp funding paid/received on both legs from recorded
  funding prints; no borrow leg (perp short).
- **Declared sensitivity grid of 4** (reported **family-wise** via the MAX-statistic at every look;
  **canonical cell binding** for the verdict): threshold ∈ {3%, 5%} × window ∈ {enter T−10/exit
  T+2, enter T−5/exit T+1}. Canonical = 3% × (T−10 → T+2). The grid is fixed here; nothing else
  is ever evaluated.

### 3.3 Power honesty (per §3)

Eligible events accrue at **~2–5/month**; the event-study needs **~100–140 powered events**, i.e.
**~3+ years** before a SURVIVE-grade verdict is even arithmetically possible. Quarterly looks report
the accruing event-study t (Newey-West, event-clustered) against the §5 boundaries; early looks are
KILL-detection (e.g., a significantly *positive* hedged short-window return at look k ≥ 4 kills the
mechanism). The dataset is the durable artifact long before the verdict.

## 4. E4 — Regime-triggered structural carry (the centerpiece)

The spec below is copied **verbatim in substance** from PROJECT_REVIEW_2026-06-09.md §5C; that
section is the normative source, frozen at commit time, all parameters justified from
already-published program numbers, **no new search**.

- **Trigger ON:** equal-weight **8-major** Binance perp funding (BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, XRP, ADA,
  DOGE, AVAX — the program's cached funding universe) **> 8%/yr sustained 30 consecutive days**
  (the program's own published "rich and rising" threshold), **AND** BTC quarterly basis annualized
  **> 5%**.
- **Book while ON:** delta-neutral, **unlevered**: long spot + short dated quarterly (**BTC + ETH,
  hold-to-convergence**) as the **senior sleeve**, plus a short-perp funding-receiver sleeve on the
  8 majors, equal-weight, **capped at 50% of book notional**; financing/borrow charged on full
  notional; taker 4 bps/side; weekly mark.
- **Trigger OFF:** funding **< 5%/yr for 30 days**, or basis **< 2%** — close at the next roll.
- **Crash-tail control (pre-declared):** the perp-spot carry KILL showed the short-crash-option
  profile (skew −12.9; Nov-2024 −18.9% month). Hence the 50% perp-leg cap with dated-basis (which
  converges contractually) as the senior sleeve. The tail stress is part of the published spec, not
  a post-hoc excuse.
- **Scoring (the claim):** "triggered windows beat T-bills net of everything, at honest **N=1**" —
  scored **only over ON-windows, cumulative across regimes**, with the **calendar-reanchor +
  regime-placebo null** (random alternative trigger dates with matched ON-duration). The
  **XS-shuffle is documented here as the *wrong* null** for a directional carry book — there is no
  cross-sectional selection claim to destroy; shuffling assets within the book leaves the harvested
  premium largely intact and proves nothing. Quarterly looks under the §5 schedule.
- **Power honesty:** in-regime carry at 2021/2024 richness paid double-digit APR on a delta-neutral
  book (dated-basis low-funding-regime Sharpe 2.87; 2024 funding blowout 10.99%/yr) → in-regime
  true Sharpe ≥ 2 → **~1–2 years of cumulative ON-time to 80% power** (§3 table, SR=2.0 row). E4 is
  the only family member that can legitimately SURVIVE on a practical horizon — but only in
  triggered time.
- **Trigger state TODAY (2026-06-09): OFF.** 2026-YTD equal-weight 8-major funding is ≈ **−0.05%/yr**.
  The hypothesis is dormant at zero cost until the regime arrives; the asymmetry is the point. The
  recorders (§6) accumulate the trigger series daily so ON-detection is mechanical, not judged.

## 5. Look schedule and alpha-spending (applies to all four)

- **Quarterly looks**, first look ≥ 13 weeks after the binding commit, then every 13 weeks.
  **Consume-once per look:** each look's score is computed exactly once, on schedule, from the
  recorder logs; no peeking between looks; out-of-schedule computations are void.
- **O'Brien-Fleming-style boundaries.** With per-hypothesis family-wise budget α\* = 0.05/4 =
  0.0125 (two-sided z_{α\*/2} = **2.498**) and **K = 8 planned quarterly looks** (a 2-year planned
  schedule per hypothesis; OPEN hypotheses re-declare a new 8-look schedule at the same α\* when it
  is exhausted), the boundary at look k is:

  **z_k ≈ z_{α\*/2} · √(K / k) = 2.498 · √(8 / k)**

  | Look k (quarter) | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 |
  |---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
  | Boundary z_k | 7.065 | 4.995 | 4.079 | 3.532 | 3.159 | 2.884 | 2.670 | 2.498 |

  (Classic OBF shape: near-unspendable early, full α\* only at the final planned look. This is the
  standard OBF approximation, not an exact Lan-DeMets spending function — declared as such.)
- **KILL is asymmetric and always allowed:** a declared kill condition firing at any scheduled look
  kills immediately; the boundaries above gate only SURVIVE claims.
- **ANY config edit = a new hypothesis.** The family count N increments (K=4 → 5, …), every
  still-OPEN hypothesis's α\* is recomputed prospectively at 0.05/K_new from its next look, and the
  edited variant starts its own 8-look schedule from zero history. Nothing is ever edited in place.
